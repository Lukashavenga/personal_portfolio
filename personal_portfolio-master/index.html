My Story

I was born in South Africa, Cape Town - and raised, mostly, in a small town called Malmesbury about 60km away. Since I was young I've had a passion for the digital world and as a small boy staring with big eyes at my first Blizzard cinematic, that passion flourished, and from that day I knew I was going to become a graphic designer! - I only later learned that's not what a graphic designer does :/ , but none the less I had discovered a new world in which anything could be possible. As a kid I didn't have any real access to programming or coding alike, I first witnessed the internet on a pc at the age of 16 from a dial-up connection on our farm, which needless to say ... actually blew my mind :) , from there on it was endless days of loading data to my cellphone just so I could tether a connection out to this vast universe and my life of gaming an South Park began. When School came to an end I needed to decide what I was going to do with my life, up to this point the suit inside of me was set on Civil or Mechanical Engineering, but then there was a creative side of me, the side that said I want to be a hippie and chase my dreams like they always do in the movies, (although we all knew where that gets you in real life) but rather than climbing mountains and taking photos of flowers - I wanted to create amazing digital content. And so that's what I did, I enrolled for Software Engineer at CTI. And even though it was expensive and the funding would be though, that's what I set my sails too and I've never been disappointed.
